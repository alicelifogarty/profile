About

When I got engaged, I was excited about the prospect of starting the next chapter of my life - both with the love of my life and with my newfound interest in design and web development. A self-starter, I decided to make my wedding an opportunity to delve into this new world. This project helped me explore aesthetics, leading me to both handlettering and web design.

Monogram

As I was developing our save the dates, I was drawn to personalized monograms and the symbolic unification of our initials. In the process, I discovered hand lettering and how to clean up the product in Adobe Illustrator.
